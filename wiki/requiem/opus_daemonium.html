Opus Daemonium

Opus Daemoniums are items that allow a Player to activate or deactivate Requiem's death mechanics, by respectively awakening or destroying a Player's demon.

There are 3 variants of Opus Daemoniums: Neutral, Curse and Cure.

Variants

Neutral

Neutral Opus Daemoniums are the base to obtain the Curse and Cure variants. Using one allows a Player to write a phrase inside it. If the phrase is correct, the book can be signed in exchange of 5 experience levels, and converted into one of the two variants, depending on the content written.

Curse

Curse Opus Daemoniums allow a Player to wake their demon. Therefore, when used, they activate the mod's death mechanics.

To convert a Neutral Opus Daemonium into a Curse one, one must write "Ad vitam aeternam" inside it and sign it.

Cure

Cure Opus Daemoniums allow a Player to destroy their demon. Therefore, when used, they deactivate the mod's death mechanics.

To convert a Neutral Opus Daemonium into a Cure one, one must write "Ad vitam mortale" inside it and sign it.
